Pediatric Dermatology

Dr.Preethi Nagaraj has earned her fellowship in pediatric dermatology, a branch of Dermatology dealing with skin disorders in children. Children are unique and so is their skin. Hence the diagnosis and treatment of skin diseases related to children requires special expertise.

  • Atopic dermatitis
  • Impetigo
  • Alopecia areata
  • Molluscum contagiosum
  • Birthmarks
